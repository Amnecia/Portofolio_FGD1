About

Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint is a South Korean web novel, adapted into a webtoon and potentially a film series, that follows the story of Kim Dokja, an office worker obsessed with a web novel titled "Three Ways to Survive the Apocalypse." When the events of the novel abruptly become reality, Dokja is the only one who knows what's coming, due to his thorough reading of the story.
Armed with this omniscient knowledge, Dokja navigates the dangerous and fantastical world as it unfolds, using his foreknowledge to survive and potentially rewrite the fate of the characters and the world itself. The story explores themes of survival, adaptation, and the power of knowledge in a world thrown into chaos.
